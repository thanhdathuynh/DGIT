What is DGIT?

DGIT is a specialized database tool designed to help researchers and clinicians explore the relationship between genes, drugs, and proteins in the context of Major Depressive Disorder (MDD) research. Using data from the DGIdb (Drug-Gene Interaction database), this tool makes it easy to discover how specific drugs interact with genes that may influence treatment outcomes.

Whether you're looking to understand a specific gene-drug interaction, explore available treatments, or dive deeper into the biomedical mechanisms behind MDD, DGIT provides the insights you need with an integrated AI assistant to help explain complex relationships.
